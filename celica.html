“The car in front is a Toyota” (1980s-2004)

Toyota Celica VII

The seventh-generation Toyota Celica, released in 1999, was a radical departure from its predecessors. It became lighter at about 2500 lbs, got an entirely new exterior design, and was equipped with two new engines a 1.8-liter inline-four (1ZZ-FE) and (2ZZ-GE).

The U.S. would get the GT and GTS variants. In Japan, the SS-I and SS-II designations would continue to be used, whereas in Europe, the VVT-I and VVTL-i valve timing systems will be used to denote the various models. As of 2006, the Celica has reached the conclusion of its seventh generation.

  • Engine :1.8 liter (inline 4)
  • Horsepower :141 hp
  • Drivetrain :RWD
  • Top Speed :205 Km/h - 127 Mph
  • 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) :8.7 s
