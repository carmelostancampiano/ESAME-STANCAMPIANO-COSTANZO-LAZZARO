Honda

Founded in 1948 in Hamamatsu, Japan, Honda opened its first U.S. storefront in Los Angeles, CA, in 1959. What began as a nimble operation with eight industrious associates would quickly grow to astounding heights For nearly four decades Honda has challenged to exceed government requirements emissions vehicle technology that led the state of California to adopt new, more stringent emissions regulations.

Honda is well known for producing inexpensive, reliable cars such as the Civic, Prelude, and Accord. While the company is well known for its more standard mass market cars, it also has a proud racing history, having featured in a wide range of ultra competitive racing categories. Notable sporting models that Honda has produced include the Civic Type R, the S800, and the highly regarded S2000.
