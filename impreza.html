Subaru Impreza WRX STI

The traditional blue and yellow Subaru is nearly synonymous with rally racing. The Subaru World Rally Team has been active since 1980 and has employed a “who’s who” list of rally-driving greats including Ari Vatanen, Colin McRae, and Petter [sic] Solberg.

It is powered by the turbocharged and intercooled 2.5-liter flat-four that cranks out more than 300 horsepower [sic]. Subaru’s invincible all-wheel drive system provides grippy traction on any surface.

  • Engine :2.0 liter (Boxer 4)
  • Horsepower :278 hp
  • Drivetrain :AWD
  • Top Speed :244 Km/h - 152 Mph
  • 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) :5.5 s
