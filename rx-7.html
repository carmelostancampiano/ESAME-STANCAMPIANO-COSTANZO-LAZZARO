“It Just Feels Right.” (1990–1995)

Mazda RX-7 III (FD)

The 3rd generation Mazda RX-7, identified by chassis code FD, was produced between 1992 and 2002, although it was only available in the US from 1993 through 1995. This generation was a departure for the model, as it was only offered with a twin-turbocharged engine.

Transmissions offered include a standard 5-speed manual and a four-speed automatic, although the latter had smaller production numbers. For the Japanese Domestic Market (JDM), notable variants include the Type R Bathurst, and the RX-7 Spirit R, produced for the final year in 2002. Samples with substantial body or engine modifications can be found in the RX-7 FD Custom market.

The 3rd generation RX-7 was the last for the model with no direct replacement, although the Mazda RX-8 is often seen as a spiritual successor.

  • Engine :1.3 liter (Wankel 2)
  • Horsepower :252 hp
  • Drivetrain :RWD
  • Top Speed :250 Km/h - 155 Mph
  • 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) :5.3 s
