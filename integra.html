Honda Integra III

The third generation of Honda Integra was introduced for the 1994 model year. The 3rd Gen Honda Integra was offered in a liftback coupe or sedan body style. These engines were paired to manual or automatic transmissions, with the majority of vehicles sending drive to the front wheels.

Notably, the 3rd Gen Integra was the only generation to feature optional all-wheel drive. In addition to variants such as the Si and SiR, the 3rd Gen Integra saw the introduction of the famed Type R. Production of the 3rd Gen Honda Integra lasted until 2001, when the 4th Gen Honda Integra was introduced.

  • Engine : 1.8 liter (inline 4)
  • Horsepower :168 hp
  • Drivetrain :FWD
  • Top Speed :209 Km/h - 130 Mph
  • 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) :7.5 s
