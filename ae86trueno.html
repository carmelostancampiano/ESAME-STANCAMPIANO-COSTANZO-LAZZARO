“The car in front is a Toyota” (1980s-2004)

Toyota AE86 Trueno

The Toyota Trueno AE86 is a compact, lightweight coupe or hatchback that Toyota released in 1983 as part of the fifth-generation Toyota Corolla series. For the sake of simplicity, the insider-chassis code “AE86” represents the range’s 1600 cc RWD model.

Sometimes, cars can be famous thanks to some external factors, for instance, being referenced in pop culture can make a car gain tremendous popularity. This is the case, ‘Initial D’ is a very popular Japanese manga series. The lead character, Takumi Fujiwara, is a street racer, which car did he drive? A Toyota Trueno AE86. The manga series was later developed into several animated and live-action projects. In all of those projects, the AE86 never failed to make an appearance. The car has also appeared in other movies, series, and video games over the years which just made it all the more popular.

  • Engine :1.6 liter (inline 4)
  • Horsepower :128 hp
  • Drivetrain :RWD
  • Top Speed :195 Km/h - 121 Mph
  • 0 to 100 km/h (0 to 62 mph) :8.9 s
