“The car in front is a Toyota” (1980s-2004)

Toyota MR2

In the US, along with the less common automatic version, it was powered by the naturally aspirated Toyota 2.2 liter 5S-FE engine capable of 130 hp via a 5-speed manual transmission. It was mostly available in a T-top version, and a small number in a coupe body style.
